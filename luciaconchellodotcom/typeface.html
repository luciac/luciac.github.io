danger-retro typeface

about the typeface

Danger-Retro is a strong and bold sans serif typeface designed for vintage construction signs from the early half of the 1900's. Based on the idea of strong industrialism and photographs of old signage, this typeface was born. Although designed for old construction/road signage, it still fits in with bold and slightly aggressive designs. A sample of it used in sports is below.

the many faces of ‘retro danger’

Although designed for old construction/road signage, it still fits in with different styles of design. Its bold and rigid structure works just as well outside its original era in far more modern design. Retro Danger is a perfect display typeface for dramatic and bold headlines.
