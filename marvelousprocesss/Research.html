MAKING THE MARVEL CINEMATIC UNIVERSE INTERFACE

Research

Information + Inspiration

It was important to start collecting the facts that make up this magnificent world. As well as find out how other cinematic worlds handle this issue of representing their information. So I did three things. I scoured the internet for awesome websites, started watching all of the films over again, and made my "marvel sketchbook" to collect all of my findings. Starting from the front I collected data. Starting from the back I sketched inspiration. One day they will meet in the middle to form the final final product.

Boilind down the information

After figuring out what people wanted to know and how the internet handled like information, I saught to organize the information people wanted to know into larger categories since its more user friendly to have less options. It came down to 5 the 5 Ws.

who – characters

what – characters

where – locations

when – timeline

why – infinity stones
